FAQs

What is ChainPortal?

ChainPortal is a streamlined platform that allows you to manage NFTs and tokens across blockchains with low fees and user-friendly tools for creating and managing assets securely.

What blockchains are currently supported?

Currently, ChainPortal supports the Solana blockchain. Additional blockchains will be integrated in the future to offer users more flexibility.

What file types are supported for NFTs?

ChainPortal supports a wide range of file types, including images, 3D objects, videos, and audio files, providing flexibility for your creative needs.

How long does the minting process take?

Minting typically completes within seconds, but the duration may vary depending on the blockchain selected and network conditions.

Is my metadata secure?

Yes. Metadata is securely stored on IPFS, ensuring decentralized and tamper-proof storage in line with Web3 principles.

What happens if the minting process fails?

If the minting process fails, your funds will be returned automatically to your wallet after deducting the applicable fee(s). You can retry the process after checking for errors or network conditions.

Where can I find my minted tokens or NFTs?

Once minted, your tokens or NFTs will be sent directly to your connected wallet, where you can view them immediately.
